What Counts as an Emergency?

If it's any of these, call us — don't wait until morning.

  • Burst or leaking pipe
  • No heat in winter
  • No AC in a heatwave (elderly / infant)
  • Water heater flooding
  • Sewer backup
  • Gas smell or suspected gas leak
  • Frozen pipes
  • Overflowing toilets that won't stop
  • Water damage in progress
  • Furnace producing carbon monoxide alarm

Gas leak? Leave the building, call 911, then call us.
